THE LARGEST BORNEO WILD AGARWOOD GALLERY IN MALAYSIA 

Sheng Dao Agarwood Art Gallery is Malaysia's largest and most reputable wild agarwood gallery consisting of an art meseum gallery and exhibition hall. Situated strategically 15 minutes away from Kota Kinabalu International Airport,  Sheng Dao Agarwood Art Gallery established their foundation within the Oceanus Waterfront Mall located next to the renowned Waterfront Kota Kinabalu. The exhibition hall of 7,ooo square feet space provides ample and optimal conditions for exhibition showcases displaying all kinds of Borneo's natural wild agarwood and exquisite wild agarwood sculptures crafted by renown local Sabahan scuptor. Sheng Dao Agarwood Art Gallery also showcases agarwood accessory products such as Agarwood bracelet, Agarwood incense stick and powder, Pure essential , Agarwood aroma oil, Agarwood fragrance, Agarwood tea and lots more.

盛道是全马来西亚最大型及富盛名的野生沉香美術館，收藏館，陳列廰。 坐落在沙巴亞庇海濱廣場（Oceanus Waterfront Mall），距離亞庇國際機場約十五分鐘的車程。 全面積七千餘方呎的展覽空間，展示著全南洋各區天然野生態的沉香；又展出數千件自然結香野生沉香原木；及展覽出沙巴名家雕塑師，精緻創作藝術沉香雕刻作品。 本藝術館設有專櫃促銷沉香手串，沉香香末及香條，沉香自然香水，沉香精煉純精油和養生沉香茶等眾多產品。
